How To Get The Most Out Of This Course
--------------------------------------
- Tips on the best way to learn
    - Watch the videos
        - At preferred speed using Udemy's playback controls
        - Slow down or rewing videos on concepts that are NOT clear

    - Code along
        - Implement the code on your own prior to looking at the author's solution        
        - NOTE: This is the best way of practice
        
    - Practice 
        - Do the assignments, practice projects & quizzes

    - Debug & search
        - Debug your code
            - Search for errors & solutions
        
        - Compare the attached codebase with your work
            - Replace your code step-by-step with the author's
            - To narrow down the root cause of an encountered issue

    - Ask & answer
        - Using Udemy's "Q&A" section to ask questions & help others

        - You will learn the most by
            - Solving issues on your own
            - Helping others
