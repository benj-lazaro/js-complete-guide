Quiz 05: undefined, null & NaN
------------------------------
- When is the undefined value coming into play?
    - Answer: Declared variables where you haven't assigned a value yet are "undefined"


- Is undefined exactly the same as null?
    - Answer: No


- When do you get NaN as a result?
    - Answer: When running a mathematical calculation with invalid inputs
        (e.g. mixing numbers and text that can't be converted to a number)


- What does typeof 'Hi there' yield?
    - Answer: "string"
