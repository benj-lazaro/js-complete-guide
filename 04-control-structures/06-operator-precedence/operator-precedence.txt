Understanding Operator Precedence
---------------------------------
- Operator precedence describes the order on which operators are executed
    - Value yieled by a higher precedence operator serves as an operand to lower precedence operator

    - Reference (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Operators/Operator_Precedence#table)

    - Reference (https://www.w3schools.com/js/js_precedence.asp)


- Mathematical operators follows the PEMDAS precedence
    - Parenthesis
    - Exponentiation
    - Multiplication
    - Division
    - Addition
    - Subtraction


- Logical operators
    - NOT   = !
    - AND   = &&
    - OR    = ||
