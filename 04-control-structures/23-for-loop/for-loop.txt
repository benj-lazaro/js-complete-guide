The for Loop
------------
- The for loop creates a loop that consists of three optional expressions
    - Enclosed in parethesis ( )
    - Separated by a colon , 
    - Followed by (usually a block) statement to be executed in the loop

    - Reference (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Statements/for)


Syntax:

for (initialization; exit_condition; afterthought/counter_variable)
    statement
