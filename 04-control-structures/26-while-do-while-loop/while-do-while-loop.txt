The while & do while Loop
-------------------------
- The while statement creates a loop that
    - Executes a specified statement as long as the test condition evalutes to true
    - The condition is evaluated before executing the statement

    - Reference (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Statements/while)


Syntax:

while (condition)
  statement


- The do while statement create a loop that
    - Executes a specified statement until the test condition evaluates to false
    - The condition is evaluated after executing the statement
        - Resulting in the specified statement executing at least once

    - Reference (https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Statements/do...while)


Syntax:

do
  statement
while (condition);
