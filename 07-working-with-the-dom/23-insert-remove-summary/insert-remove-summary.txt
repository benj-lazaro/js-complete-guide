Insertion & Removal Summary
---------------------------
- Older insertion & removal methods that have broad browser support
    - appendChild()
    - insertAdjacentElement()
    - replaceChild()
    - removeChild()


- Modern insertion & removal methods that are NOT supported by ALL browsers
    - append()
    - prepend()
    - before()
    - after()
    - replaceWith()
    - remove()

    - NOTE: Use these if Internet Explorer and/or Safari browser support is NOT a concern
